Ancient History

Born in blood and destined by fate, the Ghost of Sparta rose from mortal rage to defy the divine. Betrayed by Olympus, Kratos carved his legend across realms slaying gods, titans, and monsters alike. From the ashes of vengeance to the frostbitten wilds of new worlds, his saga echoes through time a myth forged in fury, and etched in the stars.

Kratos God of War

Kratos, the Ghost of Sparta scarred by betrayal, fueled by vengeance. A god-slayer forged in chaos, his fury shattered Olympus and echoed across realms. He is not just a warrior, but a storm in flesh unrelenting, unstoppable, unforgettable.
