Jujutsu Kaisen Main Characters

Gojo Satoru

Gojo Satoru is one of the main protagonists of the popular anime Jujutsu Kaisen. He is also the strongest curse user or Jujutsu Sorcerer in the world. His powers rival those of the King of Curses, Sukuna. Born on December 7th, 1989, his birth altered the course and balance of the world.

Sukuna Ryomen

Ryomen Sukuna, also known as Sukuna, is a powerful cursed spirit and the main antagonist in the anime and manga Jujutsu Kaisen. He was once a legendary evil sorcerer with four arms and two faces during Japan's Heian period. His soul persists within his twenty severed fingers

Megumi Fushiguro

Megumi Fushiguro is the deuteragonist in Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a Second Grade Jujutsu sorcerer and a Tokyo Jujutsu High School freshman, along with Nobara Kugisaki and Yuji Itadori. He is a descendant of the Zen’in Clan, as he is Toji Fushiguro’s son and Tsumiki Fushiguro’s step-brother.

Nobara Kugisaki

Nobara Kugisaki is one of the main characters in the Japanese manga and anime series, Jujutsu Kaisen. She is a first-year student at Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College, a training ground for Jujutsu sorcerers who battle against curses - supernatural beings that threaten humanity.

Yuta Okkotsu

Yuta Okkotsu is a first-year student at Tokyo Metropolitan Curse Technical College. He is the main protagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen 0, a prequel to the main series. Yuta is a shy and reserved character who struggles with his past traumas..

Toji Fushiguro

Toji Fushiguro is the main antagonist in the anime and manga series, Jujutsu Kaisen, who is a notorious assassin, known as the Witch Killer. Not only that, he is also a former member of the Zeni family, which is one of the three main jujutsu family clans. Toji has a confident, calm, and cold nature.
